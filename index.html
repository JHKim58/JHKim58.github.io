How do plants reorganize chromatin in response to changing environments?

Our lab studies how environmental signals reshape chromatin architecture and epigenetic states to regulate plant growth and adaptation.

Research Focus

We investigate the molecular mechanisms of plant environmental sensing, focusing on chromatin remodeling and 3D genome organization using Arabidopsis as a model system.
